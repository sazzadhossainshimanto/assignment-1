The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in
renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Dr. Muhammad Yunus, Nobel Peace Prize laureate and founder of Grameen Bank, is a globally renowned economist and social entrepreneur. His pioneering work in microfinance has lifted millions out of poverty and empowered marginalized communities, especially women. As a visionary leader, Dr. Yunus champions sustainability, social justice, and innovation, advocating for a "world of three zeros"—zero poverty, zero unemployment, and zero carbon emissions. With his non-partisan stature, proven leadership, and commitment to inclusive development, Dr. Yunus is a unifying figure capable of guiding Bangladesh through a fair and transparent transition as Chief Adviser. His focus on youth empowerment, grassroots progress, and global partnerships makes him an ideal choice to lead the nation toward a sustainable and equitable future.
